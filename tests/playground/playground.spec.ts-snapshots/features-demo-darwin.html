Article 2. SCOPE OF SERVICES

Section 1. Engagement Overview

Hargrove engages Meridian to perform the following 3 (three) services:

(1) 1st Service Engagement: Digital Transformation Assessment

Comprehensive audit of existing IT infrastructure and roadmap development. This engagement is scoped for 3 (three) months and carries a fixed fee of $48,000.00 (Forty eight thousand dollars).

This service constitutes the foundational assessment upon which subsequent engagements depend.

(2) 2nd Service Engagement: Systems Integration

End-to-end integration of ERP and supply chain management platforms. This engagement is scoped for 8 (eight) months and carries a fixed fee of $124,000.00 (One hundred twenty four thousand dollars).

(3) 3rd Service Engagement: Change Management & Training

Organizational change facilitation and staff enablement programs. This engagement is scoped for 4 (four) months and carries a fixed fee of $31,500.00 (Thirty one thousand five hundred dollars).

This service shall commence no earlier than the completion of the Systems Integration engagement described above.

Section 2. Aggregate Scope

The combined scope encompasses 15 months across 3 distinct service lines, representing a total Engagement Fee of $203,500.00.
