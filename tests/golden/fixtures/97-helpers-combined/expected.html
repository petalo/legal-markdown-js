Mutual Non-disclosure Agreement

This Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement (the "Agreement") is entered into on February 01, 2026 by and between North Atlantic Biotech Ltd ("Disclosing Party") and SIERRA LEGAL ANALYTICS LLC ("Receiving Party").

l. Term

The confidentiality obligations commence on 2026-02-01 and continue until 2030-02-01.

Either party may terminate negotiations with 45 days written notice, with a notice deadline of 18/03/2026 for this initial cycle.

l. Obligations

The Receiving Party shall comply with the following obligations:

  • Use confidential information only for evaluating the transaction.
  • Restrict disclosure to employees with a strict need to know.
  • Apply industry-standard technical and organizational safeguards.
  • Notify the disclosing party promptly after unauthorized access.

l. Financial Protections

Material disclosure threshold: 250,000 €.

Contractual penalty per verified breach: 22,500.00 €.

Maximum reimbursable expenses after reduction: 102,500.00 €.

l. Jurisdiction and Compliance

This Agreement is governed by the laws of Spain, and the courts of Madrid shall have exclusive jurisdiction.

Cross-border transfer is authorized, subject to documented transfer impact assessments.
