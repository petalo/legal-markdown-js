# 1. Parties and Purpose

This Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by Blue Harbor Technologies, Inc. and Straße Legal Innovations GmbH as of 2026-01-15.

##   1. Evaluation Purpose

The parties intend to evaluate a potential commercial collaboration involving legal automation services.

# 2. Confidential Information

##   1. Scope of Confidential Information

Confidential Information means all non-public financial, legal, technical, and operational materials disclosed by either party.

##   2. Exclusions

Residual knowledge independently retained by personnel is not deemed Confidential Information.

# 3. Permitted Use and Protection

##   1. Use Restriction

Each party shall use Confidential Information solely for the Evaluation Purpose.

##   2. Standard of Care

Each party shall protect Confidential Information with at least the same degree of care used to protect its own confidential information, and never less than reasonable care.

# 4. Term and Return of Materials

##   1. Confidentiality Term

The confidentiality obligations remain in force for 3 years from the Effective Date.

##   2. Return or Destruction

Upon written request, each party shall promptly return or destroy all Confidential Information, except archival copies maintained solely for legal compliance.
