# 1. Master Agreement

This framework sets the baseline compliance obligations.

##   1. Definitions

Defined terms apply consistently throughout this schedule.

###     1. Confidential Information

Confidential Information includes all non-public legal and technical records.

####       1. Technical Annex

This annex captures implementation controls for audit readiness.

#####         1. Security Controls

Security controls shall be reviewed quarterly by counsel and engineering.

######           1. Regional Addendum

Regional obligations supplement, but do not replace, global requirements.

#######             1. Country-Specific Compliance

Country-level filing and retention duties are mandatory where applicable.

########               1. City Schedule

Municipal disclosure and permit obligations are tracked in this schedule.

#########                 1. Site-Specific Procedure

Each site shall maintain a signed local procedure aligned with this agreement.
